About ITB

Institute of Technology, Blanchardstown (ITB) is a third-level institution which was established in 1999. It is located within the Business and Technology Park in Blanchardstown Road. ITB provides full-time and part-time higher education courses in a wide range of subjects, making awards at levels from higher certificate, to PhD.
